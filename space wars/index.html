Instructions

W - accelerate forward
A - accelerate left
S - accelerate backward
D - accelerate right
Q - rotate counter clockwise
E - rotate clockwise

↑ - move up
← - move left
→ - move right
↓ - move down

Z - fire bullet
X - fire rocket
C - fire laser
